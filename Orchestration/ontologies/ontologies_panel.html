Export to OWL / RDF

Render the selected ontology as a W3C-compatible OWL document. Vera entities → owl:Class, relationships → owl:ObjectProperty, attributes → owl:DatatypeProperty. OWL extensions (subClassOf, equivalentClass, disjointWith, Restriction, characteristics) and SKOS extensions (prefLabel, altLabel, broader/narrower) are emitted natively when present.
